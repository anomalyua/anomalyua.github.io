Veterans

    3500+ veterans in our community



    1000+ veteran care specialists (psychological, medical, social services)



    Supported by multiple nations

Drinkin' Bros is an international veterans organization and we run the Ukrainian chapter.
Come grab a drink with us! We offer a cool environment to meet with other veterns like yourself.
 Don't drink? It's okay - have something non-alcoholic! :) The important thing that we don't drink alone!
We also help to connect vets with caretakers, social workers, and psychological support.
We have organized a network of 1000+ veteran care specialists from all over the country to help assist veterans with their transition to civilian life and recovery after returning from war.

Are you or a loved one a veteran who would like to help make Ukraine a better place? If so, join our volunteer team today!
